Displacement

Displacement journeys to explore the consistency of growing textures, creating an illusionary perception of depth. The lighting coupled with the gradation of color gives a more forceful dimension, reflecting a 3D environment.

Metaball

Inspired by a Cinema 4D tutorial, Metaball explores the realm of sultry textures, warm color palettes, and gentle motion. This piece's fluidity in movement helps to excel its dynamism as a whole.
