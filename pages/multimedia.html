Senior Project

For my senior project I created 3d animations in C4D and followed up by combining Processing code with a Makey Makey, and using that in conjunction with the projection mapping software, MadMapper, to create a fully immersive & interactive piece. My thought processed concerning the animations were focused mainly on lush textures, and subtle movements. I knew I wanted to also create a certain element of illusion with the projections. While this was my first time projection mapping, I had a basic understanding of the strength and power behind it. I chose to keep simplicity within my aspect of illusion and chose a hollowed half sphere as my projection surface. As I gravitate towards fluid, spherical objects, I wanted my surface to have clear communication with my animations. I sought out a spherical surface and ended up finding a half-domed old skylight. I thoroughly cleaned the surface then spraypainted multiple white matte coats of paint on top to ensure the optimal retinal quality for the viewer. While trying projections on both the concave and convex portion of the sphere, I landed at using the concave side of it for showcasing purposes. The element of illusion played out beautifully here as the spherical shape of the surface matched my rounded animations nicely. Although the surface was hollow and concave, it appeared to be rounded and convex as a result of the 3D animations being projected onto it.
